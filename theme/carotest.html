Using Bottle


Hello! This is a quick introduction to the Bottle platform



What is Bottle?

Bottle is a messaging platform. Once you're given a phone number, you can communicate with your customers through your Bottle account. You can send texts, make calls, post pictures, and invoice customers.

Our goal is to provide you and your customers the most accessible channel possible for communicating and filling orders.

How do I create an account?

You need to contact us in order to create an account.

We will give you your phone number and get you started with the product!

How do I sign in?

To sign in, enter your Bottle phone number and password on this page.

If you don't have these credentials yet, you'll need to create an account first.

How do I communicate with customers?

Your customers can text, call, and order with you simply by having your Bottle phone number. These communications will appear online on your Bottle account!

When a customer contacts you for the first time, a new conversation will be created. Conversations are associated with the phone number you are contacting or (if the person has a Bottle account as well) the user name of the account you are contacting. You can sort through these conversations on the left-hand side of your Bottle account screen. You can also create a new conversation by clicking "New Conversation" and entering the phone number of the person you'd like to interact with.

To send a text, simply select a conversation, click the "Messages" tab, type your message, and click "Send".

To call someone, select a conversation and click the "Call" button at the top of your screen.

Can you send and receive images with Bottle?

Yes! You can attach a picture - or any other file - to a Bottle message by clicking the "Attach" button. This button is located directly beside the field in which you enter the text of your messages.

You can also view all of the files that have been sent between you and a specific customer by clicking on the "Media" tab on a conversation page.

What is a blast?

Blasts are texts to more than one person. If you want to make an annoucement to all of the customers that you communicate with using Bottle, a blast is the perfect tool.

You can send blasts by clicking your name in the top right corner of your account screen and selecting "Blast" from the drop-down menu.

How do I fill orders with Bottle?

Ordering over text is the main, most important feature of Bottle. Customers will text your Bottle account identifying exactly what product he or she would like to order. Your account records all of these requests for you, and you can invoice customers for their orders from inside your conversations with them.

To create a new order for a specific customer, click the "New Order" tab in your conversation with him or her.

Simply fill in the blanks with the details of the order and press "Invoice User". This button will not appear until the order total is greater than $0.50 to ensure invoices aren't submitted incomplete.

To view all orders from a particular customer, click the "Orders" tab in your conversation with him or her.
